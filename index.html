Virtual TerribleHack

The Government of TerribleHack has decided to move all aspects of life online, including TerribleHack itself.

Join us May 16-17!

What is TerribleHack?

Most hackathons are about coming up with innovative new ideas and making plausible startup prototypes. This one is different. This one is about having fun! Take some time to make your bad ideas a reality.

Take a look at past demos on YouTube or one of our many old Devposts for inspiration.

How it's going to work

You'll have the weekend to work on a project. Chat on Slack with us as you go. On Sunday evening, we'll join together for a video call to demo our projects to each other.
